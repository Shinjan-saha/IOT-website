LED Distance Indicator


This Project measures the distance of the object from the sensor and is indicated by how many led,s are emitted

Components used in the project:-

  • Arduino uno R3
  • Led lights
  • 1 ohm resisitor
  • Ultrasonic sensor
  • Breadboard
  • jumper wires(male-male)
  • laptop

Construction of the project

At first we set our connections we take our Breadboard , we connect our led,s we put our negeative side of led in negeative side of breadboard & positive side in any other side of breadboard ,then we set 1 ohm resitance in series with respect to position of our led.then we take our jumper wires and connect them across our resistor in similarly way we conncted our resisitor to led After setting this connection we take our ultrasonic sensor & put them in breadboard such a way that it is not across the resistor/Led's. Now we take our Arduino board & connect all the jumper wires across resistor & connect them to digital pin 2-8 and now we connect our jumper wires from ultrasonic sensor to arduino board we put VCC to 5v,GND to GND,trig to pin 11 & echo to pin 12,we connect our GND in arduino to negeative side of breadboard where we previously connected our led's negeative side

Working of the project

Now to explain the working of the project we need to look into structure of our code.We at first set our variables for the pin & must remember our connection we made in "Construction of the project" section and set our variable according to pin number. for ex:- I set trig(component of ) to pin 11, you can set it in any pin you want and just make sure your connection is according to that. After setting the variable set the input & output pin according to connection such as trig will give output & echo will take input & all th leds will be output . Set distance & durtation use the appropiate formula, now we will set the loop where acoording to distance from the object the amount of led will be turned on. Such as when the distance is 20 cm from the sensor only 2 led will be turned on now set loops for leds wheather they will be turned on or off and set the distance as the condition. Distance can be according to our choice the, ultrasonic sensor used in this project can measure distance between 2cm to 400cm.
